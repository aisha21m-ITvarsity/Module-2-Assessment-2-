ANYONE BUT YOU




MOVIE INFORMATION

AGE RESTRICTION:

18

CAST:

SYNOPSIS:

The storyline follows Bea and Ben, played by Sweeney and Powell, whose initial romantic encounter turns sour after a single date. However, they reunite at a friend's wedding in Australia, where they devise a plan to make their exes jealous by pretending to be deeply in love.
