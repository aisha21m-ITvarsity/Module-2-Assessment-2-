SYNOPSIS:

WISH focuses on 17-year-old Asha in the Kingdom of Rosas, who makes a passionate plea to the stars in a moment of need, leading her to meet a living, magic star which has fallen from the sky, and together they face up to the kingdom's evil ruler, Magnifico.
