ABOUT US

Welcome to Pine City Mall, where shopping meets adventure and where every visit promises an unforgettable experience!

Spanning over 300,000 square feet, Pine City Mall boasts a diverse selection of stores, ranging from internationally renowned brands to beloved local boutiques. Whatever you're searching for, whether it's the latest fashion trends, cutting-edge electronics,unique gifts, entertainment or some family fun, you're sure to find it here!

But Pine City Mall is more than just a shopping destination, it's a place where families come together to create lasting memories. Our state-of-the-art cinema offers the latest blockbusters in stunning clarity, while our mini golf course and entertainment center provides hours of fun for visitors of all ages.

At Pine City Mall, customer satisfaction is our top priority. Our dedicated staff and owners are passionate about providing exceptional service, ensuring that every guest feels welcomed and valued from the moment they step through our doors. Whether you need assistance finding the perfect outfit or recommendations for the best dining options in the mall, our team is always here to help, with a smile on their faces.

We invite you to experience the magic of Pine City Mall for yourself. Come explore our wide array of shops, dine at our delicious eateries, and immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere that sets us apart. Thank you for choosing Pine City Mall; where shopping, dining, and entertainment come together in perfect harmony.


OUR HISTORY

The story of Pine City Mall traces back to the vision of its founders, Spencer and Maeve Reid, who saw an opportunity to create a vibrant retail destination in the heart of their community. In 1995, armed with passion and determination, they broke ground on what would soon become a cornerstone of Pine City's commercial landscape. With a focus on providing a diverse selection of stores and exceptional customer service, the Reid's set out to create more than just a shopping center... they aimed to cultivate a hub of activity where families could come together to shop, dine, and create lasting memories.

As Pine City Mall grew, so too did its reputation for excellence. Through strategic partnerships and innovative marketing initiatives, the mall quickly became a beacon for shoppers seeking quality products and unparalleled convenience. With each passing year, the mall expanded its offerings, welcoming new tenants and introducing exciting amenities such as a state-of-the-art cinema and an entertainment center. The success of Pine City Mall not only transformed the local retail landscape but also solidified its place as a beloved community institution.

Today, Pine City Mall stands as a testament to the vision and dedication of its founders. What began as a dream shared by Spencer and Maeve has blossomed into a thriving retail destination that continues to evolve with the changing needs and desires of its patrons. With a commitment to innovation, service, and community engagement, Pine City Mall looks forward to serving generations of shoppers for years to come.
