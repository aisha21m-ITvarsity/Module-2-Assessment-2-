TAYLOR SWIFT: THE ERAS TOUR




MOVIE INFORMATION

AGE RESTRICTION:

SYNOPSIS:

Taylor Swift | The Eras Tour is a concert film produced by the singer/songwriter Taylor Swift. The film documents the Eras Tour, Swift's 2023/2024 concert tour. Immerse yourself in this once-in-a-lifetime concert film experience with a breathtaking, cinematic view of the history-making tour.
